Designing Process

We at CEE BEE Design Studio adopt a comprehensive, phased approach to every project, ensuring clarity, creativity, and best outcomes for your space.

Initial Client Consultation

The process starts with a detailed client meeting. In this onboarding session, we gather your vision, discuss the scope, wish list, and clarify your functional needs and desired aesthetics.

Discussion Points:

  • Define project scope (areas involved, room usage, goals)
  • Identify client lifestyle, style preferences, and usage patterns
  • Establish project objectives and budget alignment

Site Survey and Technical Assessment

We conduct a precise site survey, recording measurements and existing conditions through photographs and, where needed, inviting technical consultants or trades to ensure accuracy.

Includes:

  • Detailed measurement and documentation of all spaces
  • Site access for company representatives or trades

Preliminary Cost Estimate

  • Once the layout is finalised, we provide indicative budget estimates based on projected requirements.
  • This estimation may change once all materials and selections are finalized.

Design Concept Presentation (Story Board)

  • Along with the detailed furniture layout, we will also share a storyboard corresponding to the proposed design.
  • This will be provided in a PPT format to help you clearly understand the concepts and ideas we have shortlisted for your property.

  • The concepts will span various design styles such as Modern, Traditional, Country Style, European, and more.
