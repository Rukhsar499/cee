CEEBEE DESIGN STUDIO

"We are a boutique interior team of experienced Interior & Exterior Design Consultants offering end-to-end interior design services in Bangalore, Kolkata, and cities across India."

Cee Bee Design Studio is an Indian homegrown interior design brand where every home is treated as a canvas and every project as a story.

Founded with the belief that design should be personal, purposeful, and professionally executed, Cee Bee began as a boutique practice and has grown into a Private Limited Company with a robust team of 40+ designers, project managers, and industry professionals.

With over 1000 residential and 400 commercial projects completed across 7 cities in India, the studio has carved a niche for itself in high-end, customised interior design. Its portfolio spans contemporary city apartments, luxury villas, heritage restorations, and thoughtfully crafted commercial spaces—each project reflecting a balance of soul, structure, and sustainability.

Cee Bee Design Studio has also been the recipient of multiple national awards, underscoring its commitment to design innovation, storytelling, and execution excellence. What truly sets the studio apart, however, is its emphasis on personalised design, superior finish quality, and institutionalised corporate processes—a rare combination in the creative industry.

At its core, Cee Bee doesn’t just design interiors; it designs dream homes, witnessing some of the happiest milestones in a homeowner’s life. For Chitralekha and her team, this emotional connection remains the greatest reward for their craft.

Renovation Services

Every renovation is a story of chaos turning into order.

At CeeBee Design Studio, renovation is about more than aesthetics—it’s about renewal. We begin by understanding the emotions, memories, and functions that make a house a home, especially when families wish to preserve legacy spaces while giving them new life. Our approach balances thoughtful design with technical precision. From curated materials and 3D visualisations to upgraded plumbing, electrical, and sanitary systems, we ensure every home is renewed from the inside out.
